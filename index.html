Why 91GI Exists

The land grows the
flavour. We just carry it.

For centuries, an Indian basmati grew its perfume because of where it stood. A Kashmir saffron stained water gold because of which hill it climbed. A Lakadong turmeric healed because of which valley it rooted into.

A Geographical Indication tag is the law's way of saying: this place, these hands, this story — they cannot be copied. 91GI is built around that promise.

Every product here is sourced direct from its GI region, paid for at a fair farmer price, and shipped with the certification papers behind it. Nothing reformulated. Nothing imitated. Nothing whispered about.
